Welcome to Akina Nation!

What is this blog?

On this blog, you can share things about your favourite cars, talk about your own cars, and much more. Akina Nation is used by people just like you, therefore, the website is an environment where you can be comfortable. From Japanese car culture such as Kaido Racers, Touge style builds, to American car culture such as drag racing, and muscle, there are so many things to talk about that you'll always learn something new and be interested about. Beside this text is a photo of racers potentially in the Wangan, in the style of the "Mid Night Club" that is now defunct.

Our website name...

The name "Akina Nation" is inspired by the anime "Initial D", we chose this name as it brings us back to the Japanese car culture of the 90s. Some may describe this era of cars as being the "golden age", with many still styling their cars the same way. The popular racing technique, "drifting" also influences the current era as it is a stylish move used to control oversteer in a skillful manner. Beside this text is a photo of the Nissan 180SX which is a popular drift car along with other "S-chassis" cars. Despite the website being inspired by Japanese car culture, it is not limited to that and users are able to talk about all types of car culture.
